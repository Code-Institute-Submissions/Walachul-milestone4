MISSION || VISION || VALUES

The mission of the Romanian Alpine Club is to develop, support, practice and promote any kind of recreational activities in nature, with the conscious minimization of risk factors and with care and respect for the environment, fauna and flora as well as nature conservation.

The vision of the Romanian Alpine Club is to develop a strong community that will become a respected voice in Romania regarding responsible practice and the promotion of all noninvasive mountain activities and respect for the natural environment.

Our values are:
We develop ethics in mountain activities. We support the permanent effort for a correct and complete mountain education. We promote performance athletes as well as table sports. We declare friendship and tolerance for attracting fellows to mountain activities.
